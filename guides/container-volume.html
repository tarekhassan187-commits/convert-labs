Container volume — boxes & cylinders

Formulas

  • Box (rectangular prism): V = length × width × height
  • Cylinder: V = π × r² × height (r = radius)

Example

Box 30 cm × 20 cm × 10 cm → V = 30×20×10 = 6000 cm³ = 6 L.

Estimate mass of liquid

Use density: mass = volume × density. For water: 6 L → ~6 kg.
