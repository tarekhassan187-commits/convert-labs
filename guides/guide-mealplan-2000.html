Grocery list (basics for the week)

  • Proteins: chicken breast, lean beef, salmon/cod, eggs, canned tuna, tofu
  • Carbs: oats, wholegrain bread, brown rice/quinoa, pasta, potatoes
  • Dairy & alternatives: Greek yoghurt, milk, cottage cheese
  • Fruits & veg: bananas, apples, berries, oranges, salad greens, broccoli, root veg
  • Pantry: olive oil, nuts, peanut butter, chickpeas, lentils, spices
  • Extras: granola, honey, maple syrup, tahini
Quick tips: scale portions by multiplying ingredient weights by your target/2000. For example, if your target = 2400 kcal, multiply each ingredient by 1.2. Keep protein first ‚Äî aim for 0.8‚Äì1.2 g/kg of bodyweight for maintenance/active users, more if building muscle.

Substitutions & dietary swaps

Meal prep & time-saving
