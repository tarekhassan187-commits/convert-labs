Common pitfalls & how to avoid them

  • Unit confusion (e.g., mixing US and metric cups vs. mass vs. volume): always confirm the base unit.
  • Rounding too early: perform calculations at full precision, round only at final display step.
  • For small values, choose smaller units (use mm for mechanical fits under 1 cm).

Practical tips for measuring

  • Use a calibrated tape or digital caliper for precise work.
  • When measuring long distances, use consistent units (e.g., metres) and break into segments if necessary.
  • Record the measurement unit with values to avoid confusion later.
