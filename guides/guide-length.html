Understanding Length Conversions

Convert between meters, centimeters, millimeters, inches, feet and miles. Learn the formulas, common pitfalls, and quick examples for real-world use.

Key formulas

  • 1 m = 100 cm
  • 1 in = 2.54 cm (exact)
  • 1 ft = 12 in = 0.3048 m (exact)

Practical tips

  • Use inches/cm for small objects, meters for room dimensions.
  • When converting, keep a consistent number of decimal places depending on tolerance.
