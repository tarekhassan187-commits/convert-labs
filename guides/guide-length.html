Understanding Length Conversions

A complete guide to converting meters, centimetres, millimetres, inches, feet and miles. Includes formulas, worked examples, diagrams, rounding advice and measurement best-practices.

1. Why length conversions matter

Length measurements are a fundamental part of everyday life ‚Äî from construction and home DIY to travel distances and scientific calculations. Modern tools make conversions instant, but knowing how the numbers actually work helps you avoid mistakes, communicate clearly, and choose the correct precision for your task.

The two main systems

  • Metric (SI): millimetres, centimetres, metres, kilometres
  • Imperial/US: inches, feet, yards, miles

2. Exact relationships you must memorise

  • 1 m = 100 cm = 1000 mm
  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm (exact, by definition)
  • 1 foot = 12 inches = 30.48 cm
  • 1 yard = 3 feet = 91.44 cm
  • 1 mile = 5280 feet = 1609.344 m
