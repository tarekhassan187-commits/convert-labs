Volume conversions & practical examples

Common conversions

  • 1 L = 1000 mL
  • 1 cup (US) = 236.588 mL
  • 1 US gallon = 3.78541 L
  • 1 UK (imperial) pint = 568.261 mL

Volume → Mass

mass (g) = volume (mL) × density (g/mL). Example: 200 mL water (density 1.0) → 200 g.

Cooking tip

For dry ingredients, use weight (grams) when possible — it's more consistent than volume for baking.
