Common pitfalls & how to avoid them

  • Multiple 'cup' definitions: US customary, metric cup (250 mL), and imperial cups differ ‚Äî always label which cup you use.
  • Chained rounding: do not round between steps. Keep raw values and round only for display.

Practical measuring tips

  • Use measuring cups for approximate kitchen work and graduated cylinders or volumetric flasks for scientific accuracy.
  • For viscous liquids, use the meniscus reading method or weigh the liquid for greater repeatability.
  • When converting bulk volumes (pallets, tanks), account for headspace and packing efficiency rather than raw internal volume alone.
