Temperature scales & conversions

Formulas

  • °C → °F: (°C × 9/5) + 32
  • °F → °C: (°F − 32) × 5/9
  • °C → K: °C + 273.15
  • K → °C: K − 273.15

Example

Convert 20°C to °F: (20 × 9/5) + 32 = 68°F.

Notes

  • Use Kelvin for scientific work; Celsius for weather; Fahrenheit still common in US.
  • Be careful with integer rounding in code — keep decimals until final display.
