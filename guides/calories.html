Calories & nutrition — how estimates work

Energy from macronutrients

  • Protein: 4 kcal/g
  • Carbohydrates: 4 kcal/g
  • Fat: 9 kcal/g

Per-100g basis

Nutrition datasets usually report values per 100 g. Serving conversions = (value/100) × serving_grams.

Example

If 100 g of food = 10 g protein, 20 g carbs, 5 g fat → energy = (10×4)+(20×4)+(5×9)=40+80+45=165 kcal per 100 g.

Limitations

  • Regional recipes vary — aggregate data may not match home-cooked versions.
  • Density and water content affect per-volume estimates (cups → g conversions).

Best practice

For accuracy, weigh servings in grams and consult labels for processed foods; use datasets (USDA) as a reference, not a medical source.
