Image formats — choose the right one

JPG (JPEG)

Best for photos. lossy compression, small size at high quality but no transparency.

PNG

Lossless, supports transparency. Good for graphics, logos; larger files.

WEBP

Modern format: better compression than JPG and PNG, supports transparency; good for web where supported.

SVG

Vector format for icons and illustrations — scalable with tiny size for simple graphics.

GIF

Animated images, limited colors; often larger than modern alternatives like animated WEBP.

Tip

For web: prefer WEBP/JPG for photographs and SVG for icons. Provide fallbacks where needed.
