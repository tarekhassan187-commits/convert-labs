Privacy Policy

How Convert Labs collects and uses data, cookies, Google services, and how we protect your privacy.

Last updated: 2025

What we collect

When you use our tools you may enter values (weight, height, age, units). These inputs are used only for on-page calculations and are not stored on our servers.

We also collect anonymous analytics data (browser, device, pages visited) to improve the site.

Cookies & third-party services

We use Google services: Google AdSense for ads and Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics. These services may set cookies or identifiers to deliver ads and measure performance.

To control ad personalization visit: Google Ad Settings.

How we use data

  • Improve site performance and tools
  • Fix bugs and errors
  • Deliver relevant ads via Google AdSense

No selling or sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share user-entered calculator inputs. Google handles ad data under its own policies.

Your choices

You can disable cookies in your browser or opt out of personalized ads at: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

GDPR & CPRA Compliance

If you are visiting from the European Union (GDPR) or California (CPRA), you have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access the data we collect about you.
  • Right to request correction or deletion of personal data.
  • Right to opt-out of data sale or targeted advertising.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@convertlabs.online.

Data Retention

We retain collected anonymous analytics data for as long as necessary to provide our services and improve the site. Calculator inputs entered on the page are not stored.
