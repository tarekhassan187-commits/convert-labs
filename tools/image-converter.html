Understanding Image Formats

JPG (or JPEG) is ideal for photos and web use because it compresses images efficiently, slightly reducing quality to save space.

PNG retains full quality and supports transparency, making it perfect for graphics, logos, and screenshots.

WEBP is a modern format created by Google ‚Äî offering smaller file sizes than JPG or PNG with excellent quality.

GIF supports simple animations but limited colors (256 only). It‚Äôs best for short clips, icons, and simple illustrations.
