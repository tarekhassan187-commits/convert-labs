Understanding Currency Exchange

Exchange rates show how much one currency is worth compared to another. For example, if 1 USD = 47.28 EGP, then 1 US Dollar equals 47.28 Egyptian Pounds.

Exchange rates fluctuate constantly due to inflation, trade, and global market demand. Real-time converters like Convert Labs help ensure accurate conversions for individuals, businesses, and travelers.
