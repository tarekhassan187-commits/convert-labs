Understanding Currency Exchange

Currency exchange rates represent how much one currency is worth compared to another. For example, if 1 USD = 47.28 EGP, that means 1 US Dollar equals 47.28 Egyptian Pounds.

Exchange rates fluctuate constantly due to market forces such as inflation, interest rates, trade, and global demand. Real-time converters help individuals, businesses, and travelers get accurate conversions for global transactions.
